CONCEPT OVERVIEW

In an attempt to minimize the risk of the spread of the new coronavirus, which has infected hundreds of thousands of people in at least 120 countries, many educational institutions are advising students to stop attending classes and closing campuses.

To solve this problem, MockBoard, a leading ed-tech startup of India has launched its own virtual classroom, MOCKDEL. MOCKDEL aims to provide students and teachers a platform for conducting online classes when they can’t come to school.

Digital whiteboard
Draw and write together on a digital white board in your virtual class to make concepts easier and interesting


Smooth live video stream
Enjoy your lecture with high quality smooth live video stream direct on your device.


Session recording and archive
MockDel provides you an option to record and archive session videos on its platform. Students can access these lectures on demand.

Real time notes
Professors can share important notes in real time with students. Students can download these notes in PDF/Word/PPT format.


Discussion forum, live polls and quizzes
MOCKDEL provides a discussion forum and options of live polls and quizzes to make lessons interesting


Manage assignment and study material
Professors can provide study material and collect assignments during or after the class without any hustle.
